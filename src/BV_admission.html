Admissions

PSG College of Technology admission process 2023 is both merit and entrance-based. The institute offers admission on the basis of entrance exams, such as TNEA and TANCET for UG Engineering and postgraduate courses. PSGCT offers over 60 full and part-time UG, PG and PhD programmes across multiple streams such as Science, Engineering, Technology, Computer Applications and Management, namely BE/ BTech, ME/ MTech, MBA, MCA, MSc and PhD. Applicants are admitted to the BE/ BTech programme through TNEA scores, while ME/ MTech admission is done on the basis of GATE or TANCET exam scores followed by TANCA Counselling. Other than the UG and PG courses, the institute also offers full-time and part-time PhD MS across all departments.
